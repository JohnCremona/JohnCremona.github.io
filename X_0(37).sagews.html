To evaluate $j=a(v_1)+w_1b(v_1)$ at the relevant (non-cusp rational) points $(v_1,w_1)=(-1,4), (1,4)$ is not so easy since its poles are at $(v_1,w_1)=(1,-4), (-1,-4)$ so the rational functions $a,b$ have poles at $\pm1$.
