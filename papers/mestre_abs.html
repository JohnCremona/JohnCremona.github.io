On a theorem of Mestre and Schoof

J. E. Cremona, Andrew V. Sutherland

Abstract

A well known theorem of Mestre and Schoof implies that the order of an elliptic curve $E$ over a prime field $\mathbb{F}_q$ can be uniquely determined by computing the orders of points on $E$ and its quadratic twist, provided that $q>229$. We extend this result to all finite fields with $q>49$, and all prime fields with $q>29$.
