Reduction of binary forms over imaginary quadratic fields

Abstract

We show how the classical theory of reduction of real binary forms with respect to the action of SL$(2,\mathbb{Z})$ may be extended to a reduction theory for binary forms with complex coefficients under the action of certain discrete groups. In particular, we give some explicit results concerning the reduction of binary cubics and quartics with coefficients in the ring of integers of an imaginary quadratic field of class number one (such as $\mathbb{Z}$[i]), and mention applications to the enumeration of cubic fields and two-descent on elliptic curves.
